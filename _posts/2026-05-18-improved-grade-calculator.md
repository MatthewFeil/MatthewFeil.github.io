## Try the improved calculator [here]({{ "/gradecalculatorv2/" | relative_url }})!

It's that time of year: finals season. It can be pretty stressful for students, but what makes it even worse is not knowing what grade you'll end up with after you take the final. A year ago, I made a grade calculator to help students calculate what grade they would need to earn on the final to get a certain letter grade for their total grade. It's pretty useful, however it was pretty limited. You were stuck with one set grading scale as well as only rounding when 0.5% or less away. I've made an improved version with a few more features, hopefully to make figuring out grades a little easier. I hope you find it helpful, and good luck on your finals!

## What's new?

- Instant results (no need to press calculate)
- Toggleable rounding on/off
- Customizable rounding percentage (within the nearest x%)
- Customizable grading scale (percentage to letter grade conversion)
- Customizable final to semester grade weighing ratio
- Enter a grade you think you'll get on the final and see what grade it'll earn you
